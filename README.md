## 📖 About the Project

> **Kyrgyzstan's tourism operates blind.** Hotels, yurts, car-sharing services, and guides run through WhatsApp and Excel. The government has no unified picture of tourist flows and revenue. Information is scattered across dozens of websites and social media pages.

**Unigency AI** is a single product that solves these problems for three audiences at once: tourists, tourism businesses, and the government.

## ✨ Key Features

- **🤖 AI Routes** — personalized tours on request in Russian
- **🗺 Interactive Map** — tour stops and days visualized
- **📦 CRM for Agencies** — tours, bookings, and analytics in one window
- **📈 End-to-End Analytics** — from click to booking
- **🔗 Integrations** — Booking, Yandex Taxi, Ticket.kg, Google services
- **✅ Registry of Legal Companies** — verified providers only

## 💰 Business Model

```
B2C  →  Commission on bookings (tours, yurts, hotels, guides, car-sharing)
B2B  →  CRM subscription + integration fees
B2G  →  Government contracts for data and analytics
```
